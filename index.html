KiwappVideo.js

Enhance your app with native video playing.

Why a native video player?

If you ever used video playing in html5, you know how it can be hard to custom the playing behaviour. Then, kiwappVideo.js provides you same API and efficiency playing video on Android and iPad.

This library needs Kiwapp.js loaded on your app to work.
